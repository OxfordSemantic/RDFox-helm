Accessing RDFox
---------------

1. Check that the RDFox pod / pods are running:

	kubectl get pods -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} -l app.kubernetes.io/instance={{ .Release.Name }}

2. Port-forward the service to your local machine:

	kubectl port-forward -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} svc/{{ include "rdfox.fullname" . | printf "%s-loadbalancer" | trunc 63 | trimSuffix "-" }} {{ .Values.service.port }}:{{ .Values.service.port }}

3. Open RDFox Console:

	{{ include "rdfox.protocol" . }}://localhost:{{ .Values.service.port }}/console
